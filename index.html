WHAT IS YOQI?

YOQI combines yoga and qigong, two of the greatest traditional systems of energy movement. Rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine, YOQI emphasizes the wisdom of the body and its vital connection to nature through breath, flow and meditation.

WHAT IS QI?

Qi means life force energy. Energy is simply potential until it's given a pattern. The pattern that you give your energy determines the shape of your life. The YOQI method is designed to help you repattern unhealthy patterns of energy and create healthy patterns of energy using safe and effective principles from yoga and qigong that you can do right at home. Whether you are a beginner, or have experience with yoga and energy arts, YOQI offers a range of levels and classes for everyone.
